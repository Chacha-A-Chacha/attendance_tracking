What Happens Next?

Here's what to expect after updating your receipt.

Verification Process

  • Your payment status will be reset to "Under Review"
  • Our admin team will verify your new receipt within 1-2 business days
  • You'll receive an email notification once verification is complete
  • Your old receipt will be replaced and no longer accessible

Security Notice

Make sure your receipt image is clear and shows all payment details. Blurry or incomplete receipts may delay verification. Include the full receipt showing payment amount, date, and transaction reference.
